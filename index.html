Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our property reset service

What if my property needs more work than the standard package?

We'll let you know during the initial consultation if your property requires additional work beyond the standard package. We can provide a custom quote for extensive repairs, or recommend specialists for major renovations if needed.

Can you work around my schedule or tenant handover dates?

Yes! We understand property turnovers work to tight timelines. We offer flexible scheduling including weekends where possible. Just let us know your deadline and we'll do our best to accommodate it.

Do you offer any guarantees on your work?

Yes, we guarantee all work for 7 days after completion. If something we've fixed comes loose or a repair fails, we'll come back and make it right at no charge. We also provide photo documentation of all completed work.

What areas do you service?

We're based in Kimberley and service the greater Kimberley area and surrounding Northern Cape towns. For properties outside Kimberley, a small travel surcharge may apply. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.

How do I get started?

Simply send us a message on WhatsApp with your property address, number of bedrooms, and any photos if you have them. We'll respond with a quote usually within a few hours. Once you accept, we'll schedule a convenient date for the work.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT, and card payments. Payment is due upon completion of work. For larger jobs, we may request a 50% deposit upfront with the balance on completion.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Send us a message on WhatsApp and we'll answer any questions you have.
